<title>Core</title>
<p id="core">This clause establishes the <strong>Core</strong> requirements class, with IRI <tt>/req/core</tt>, which has a single corresponding conformance class, <strong>Core</strong>, with IRI <tt>/conf/core</tt>. The resulting vocabulary — an ontology — can be used to construct SPARQL graph patterns for querying appropriately modeled geospatial data.
